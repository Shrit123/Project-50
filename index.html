Interesting facts about Swans

  • A male swan is called a cob, and a female swan is called a pen.
  • Swans can fly as fast as 60 miles per hour
  • The swan has over 25,000 feathers in it's body

Interesting facts about Turtles

  • The largest turtles weigh over 1000 pounds
  • Turtles have a second shell beneath their outer shell
  • Turtles are some of the oldest animals in the world
